01 The Difference

Change creates opportunity

In financial services, technology and new consumer expectations are creating spaces in which traditional banks can’t operate. Pollen Street Capital invests in businesses that thrive in these new spaces.

Our knowledge gives us access to the whole of the financial services market and we choose specialist sectors within it that offer attractive risk:return ratios.

We work closely with entrepreneurial management teams who share our values and whose businesses have strong potential for growth and market share expansion.

Thanks to deep experience in financial services, we are able to contribute market, product and process expertise as well as capital.

We are partners, not just investors.

02 Investment Strategy

Partnerships, alignment of Interests and control

We are business partners as well as capital providers. We work with experienced teams and our deals ensure alignment of interests, enduring relationships and long-term success.

Capital is allocated only to markets with attractive through-cycle returns.

Deal structure, coupled with active underwriting, results in strong downside protection and continuous event-sensitive adjustment.

03 Case Study

Shawbrook Bank. The challenger bank with 100,000 customers.

Year invested: 2011

Year exited: current invested

Sector: financial services

Type: growth capital

Shawbrook Bank is a UK challenger bank providing best in class financial products and services to under served segments of the SME and consumer sectors.

The business provides commercial mortgages, asset finance and invoice financing to SMEs and various loan types to consumers including home improvement loans, secured loans and unsecured loans.

Lending to SME’s accounts for over 70% of the lending assets, differentiating it from other challenger banks.

Shawbrook was founded in 2011 and has since grown rapidly with the balance sheet increasing from £20m to over £2bn by June 2014 with over 100,000 customers.

The balance sheet is financed through an online retail savings proposition which offers consistent rates to consumers and SMEs and has also accessed the funding for lending scheme.

06 Sustainable Investing

Responsible Policies. Ethical Operations. Sustainable Investing.

Environmental. PSC invests responsibly. As part of its pre-investment process, PSC examines the attitude to waste management, energy and sourcing of potential investments and their associated companies.

Social. We insist that labour and working conditions meet the required regulatory standards and that emphasis is given to the health and safety of employees and the social development of communities in which potential investments and their associated companies operate.

Governance. We insist on honesty, integrity, fairness and respect in all our business dealings and those of our partners.
